 KANPUR: The Times of India, in association with Allenhouse Institute of Technology, will be conducting an awareness rally on 'World AIDS Day' on Tuesday. Over 500 students from various schools of the city are likely to participate in the rally. It will be inaugurated by divisional commissioner L Venkateshwar Lu who will also address the students on the subject.   'Rally on World AIDS Day Awareness' will commence from Somdutt Plaza at 10 in the morning and will end at Phoolbagh where participating students will form a human chain in the shape of 'Red Ribbon'-- the symbol of AIDS awareness.   MD of Superhouse Group Mukhtarul Amin would also participate in the event.
