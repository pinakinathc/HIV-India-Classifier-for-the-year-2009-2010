 MYSORE: In an effort to expand awareness about AIDS, over 45 NGOs are taking part in a two-day state-level meet, Chaithra Samagama, which started here on Tuesday.   The meet being organized by Swami Vivekananda Youth Movement in association with Karnataka Health Promotion Trust will aim at sharing experiences and best practices in fighting the disease. Making health care accessible to key population and establishing link with government departments in HIV programmes will also be discussed.   On Wednesday, director of KHPT H S Ashokanand and director of department of women and child development Sandhya Venugopal Sharma will take part in deliberations.
