 ALLAHABAD: The All India Democratic Students Organisation (AIDSO) has decided to bring out a student bulletin to highlight the movement started by them for the revival of the students union and the autocratic attitude of varsity authorities.   Giving this information, AIDSO convenor Pawan Pandey said the general public should be made aware about the conspiracy to crush the students movement. As a part of this, AIDSO activists pasted photographs and newspaper clippings of police brutality on students in various hostels of the university. They also carried a signature campaign.   Pandey informed that the district president of AIDSO Raghvendra Singh has also sent a memorandum to the National Human Rights Commission (NHRC) and Indian president demanding strong action against guilty policemen and immediate restoration of students union and holding of students union polls.
