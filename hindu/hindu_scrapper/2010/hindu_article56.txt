ON A TOUR OF THE COUNTRY: The Red Ribbon Express-II, an eight-coach train on a year-long journey across the country to spread awareness on HIV, entered Karnataka through Belgaum city on Tuesday. After a formal welcome to the express train, the exhibition on board was inaugurated by Zilla Panchayat president Ashvini Patil. Suresh C. Angadi, MP, and Sanjay Patil, MLA, were present.
