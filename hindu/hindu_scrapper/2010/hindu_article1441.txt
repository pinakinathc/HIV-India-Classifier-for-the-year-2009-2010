“Coaching for Hope”, a programme to spread awareness through football coaching, was launched by Sova , a local NGO in collaboration with Skill Share International, Save the Children and DFID . While 15 tribal young boys were selected for getting training from international coaches Jacob and .Dan with a supportive hand from J. Hema Rao , a former national level player and coach from the locality for coming two years, they would also be offered specialised training on spreading the message of preventing HIV/AIDS in their communities, Sanjit Patnaik, secretary, Sova said. Ashok Kumar Pangi, president, Koraput zilla parishad, attended the programme as chief guest .
