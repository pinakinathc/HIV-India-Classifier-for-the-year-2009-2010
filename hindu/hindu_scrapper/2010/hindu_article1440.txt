Station Health Organisation of Eastern Naval Command Headquarters organised a `Red Ribbon Show' at Samudrika Naval Auditorium on Friday as part of observing the World Aids Day with Flag Officer Commanding Eastern Fleet P. Murugesan and his wife Medha attending. Students from navy Children School of Nausena Baugh performed a skit highlighting different modes of HIV transmission and prevention. A short film `Savdhan' produced by Ministry of Defence was screened.
