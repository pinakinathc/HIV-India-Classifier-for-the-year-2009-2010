Awareness campaign: Collector T. Bhaskaran flagging of the Life Bus of the Kerala State AIDS Control Society at Lakkidi in Wayanad on Friday. Â   A Correspondent KALPETTA: Life Bus, a campaign vehicle of the Kerala State AIDS Control Society to spread awareness on HIV-prevention steps, began its one-week tour in the district on Friday. District Collector T. Bhaskaran flagged off the campaign on the premises of the Oriental School of Hotel Management, Lakkidi. A. Anitha, president Vythiri panchayat, presided. The bus will stop at 25 grama panchayats and one municipality, visiting three centres daily. Video shows will be screened and artistes of the Nehru Yuvak Kendra will stage cultural programmes on all days.
