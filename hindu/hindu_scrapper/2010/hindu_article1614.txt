S. Rahul Ganesh (11), a Standard VI student of Sri Narayani Vidyalaya, Thirumalaikkodi, Vellore, donated Rs.350 from his personal savings to the family of J. Perumal of Alamaram Street in Kuppam village in Vellore taluk, whose thatched house was destroyed in a fire accident on Monday Chakravarthy, Village Administrative Officer, Ariyur, issued a certificate to Ganesh. Ganesh is the recipient of the National Child Award for Exceptional Achievement 2010 for his achievement in cart-wheeling to create awareness of social issues such as AIDS and child labour.
