K ids for Kids (KFK) presents Alive 2010, on August 20 (6.30 p.m.) at the Music Academy. This is the fourth of their concerts, and it will feature more than 40 participants in the age group of four to 25.The show will include vocal performances, a rock band and skits that accentuate the principles of living right. Kids for Kids International is a city-based social organisation founded by a group of youngsters that works towards providing disadvantaged children a better life. KFK provides education to children with HIV and extends financial support to Little Drops in Porur and educational assistance to children in the slums.
