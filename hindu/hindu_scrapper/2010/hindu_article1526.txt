Chandrasekaran, flagging off the bicycle rally in Tiruvarur. — Photo: M.Srinath    District Collector, M.Chandrasekaran flagged off a cycle rally by youth to create awareness about AIDS here on Saturday. According to Shanmugham, co-ordinator of the rally, nearly 50 youngsters started the cycle rally at Ramanathapuram and reached Tiruvarur via Sivaganga and Thanjavur. From Tiruvarur the youth will cycle to Sirkazhi in Nagapattinam district. The rally was organised by Tamil Nadu AIDS control Society.
