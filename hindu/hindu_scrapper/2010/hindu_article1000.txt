M.R.C. Mohan Rao Â   M.R.C. Mohan Rao, lecturer in Silver Jubilee College here, was adjusted the best NSS Programme Officer at university level. Out of 300 entries, Mr. Rao topped the list. He will receive the award at a function on September 24 from the Higher Education Minister. The staff and students of the college congratulated the teacher for achieving the rare distinction. Mr. Rao has been active as NSS Programme Officer in adopting remote villages for social development, rendering help to the victims during floods by coordinating with NGOs and carrying out HIV awareness programmes among youth, especially students.
