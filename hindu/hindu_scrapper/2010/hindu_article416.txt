Staff Reporter KARIMNAGAR: About 12,485 people have visited the exhibition at Red Ribbon Express train at the Karimnagar railway station on Tuesday and Wednesday. On the second day on Wednesday, Satavahana university Vice-Chancellor Md Iqbal Ali and others visited the RRE train. Students of various educational institutions, women self-help group members and others visited in large numbers to have glimpse of the exhibition on wheels. Awareness on AIDS The trained volunteers had educated the visitors about the spread of dreaded AIDS\HIV, malaria, swine flue, TB and other diseases. Voluntary organizations such as REACH, SEWS, Lodi, St Ann's, and others had also put up stalls and distributed pamphlets on the need of prevention of AIDS\HIV disease at the railway station.
