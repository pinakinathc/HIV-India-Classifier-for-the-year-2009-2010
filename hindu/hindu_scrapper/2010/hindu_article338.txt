Staff Correspondent Madikeri: People afflicted with tuberculosis must undergo HIV tests to prevent further deterioration of their health, Kodagu district TB control officer Jnanamani Nanaiah said here on Monday. He was speaking at the World TB Day function in Town Hall. He called upon patients not to discontinue the tablets prescribed, according to a press release. President of the Madikeri City Municipal Council (CMC) P.D. Ponnappa inaugurated the event and said that the youth must be involved in TB awareness programmes.
