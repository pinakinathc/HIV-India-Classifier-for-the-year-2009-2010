Youth carnival, Ilavattam, was held here on July 28 and 29, says a release from the organisers. The event, aimed at creating awareness on HIV/AIDS, saw students participate in oratorical, individual dance, group dance, street play, painting, etc. At the prize distribution ceremony, Commissioner of Police C. Sylendra Babu asked the women participants to be courageous, take up responsibility and scale greater heights in education and career. For, in their prosperity lay the nation's. B. Mohamed Ali, District Programme Officer, District AIDS Prevention and Control Unit, asked the winners to perform well at the State level.
