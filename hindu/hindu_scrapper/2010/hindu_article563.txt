Krishnarajendra Hospital, attached to the Mysore Medical College and Research Centre, received a blood transportation vehicle here on Thursday. Hospital Superintendent Geetha Avadhani received the vehicle. The Superintendent told   that the Department of Health and Family Welfare and the Karnataka AIDS Prevention Society provided the vehicle. She said the blood collected in camps could be transported to the blood bank in the hospital or the mini blood banks at the primary health centres in the district without any problem as the vehicle had four refrigerated compartments.
