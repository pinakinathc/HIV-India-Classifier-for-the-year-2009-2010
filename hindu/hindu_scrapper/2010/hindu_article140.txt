Staff Reporter VISAKHAPATNAM: The GVMC on Thursday organised an awareness programme at Nerella Koneru Centre as a part of TB Prevention Week. Corporator Bandaru Srinivasa Rao presided. District TB Control Officer Sasidhar Kumar explained the efficacy of Directly Observed Treatment, Short Course (DOTS). GVMC Medical Officer and Secretary of Visakha AIDS Control Society K.S.L.G. Sastry said those suffering from fever for more than two weeks and symptoms of TB should go for tests. Senior Medical Officer Durga Kalyani, TB Control Unit Medical Officer Santisri and nursing students of Academy of Life Sciences participated. A house-to-house campaign was carried out and tests were conducted for 14 HIV-affected persons.
