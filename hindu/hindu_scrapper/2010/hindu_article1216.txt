Narasu's Sarathy Institute of Technology, Salem conducted a Department of Public Health and UNICEF- sponsored training programme to create awareness to students for a HIV/AIDS free safe motherhood and parenthood, recently on its premises. Principal K. Raja presided over the function in which Shyamaladevi, Training coordinator spoke on awareness about public health. Dr. Sripriya, Block Medical Officer, Primary Health Centre, Kadayampatti gave a lecture on motherhood. Senthilkumar , ICTC Counselor, Kadaympatti gave a lecture on HIV/AIDS. C. Vinothkumar, NSS Programme Officer, M. Senthilkumar, YRC Programme Officer, A. Anandaraj, RRC Programme Officer made arrangements for this programme. More than 300 students got benefited.
