Rajahmundry/Tuni: The Government is contemplating to make Anti-Retroviral Therapy (ART) mandatory for HIV-infected prisoners, K. Chandramouli, Director General of National AIDS Control Organization (NACO) said here on Monday. Talking to reporters at Tuni and Rajamundry Central Prison after a visit along with NACO team and AP State AIDS Control Society (APSACS) officials, he said the prevalence of HIV was highest in the State in the country with the State accounting for 5.2 lakh of the 30 lakh HIV/AIDS cases.
