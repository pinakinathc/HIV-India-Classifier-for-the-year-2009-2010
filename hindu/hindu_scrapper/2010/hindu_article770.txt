Collector Ramsankar Naik proposed subsidised ration for HIV positive people under Anthyodaya Annapurna Yojana scheme and social pension from the DRDA programme. Addressing a meeting of health officials here on Thursday, he said HIV patients required nutritious diet to fight the infection. He also said bus passes to the patients and attendants at 50 per cent fare was planned by the government. He said besides the doles, the HIV positive persons should be given training in skills such as tailoring and other vocations so that they could stand on the own for some time.
