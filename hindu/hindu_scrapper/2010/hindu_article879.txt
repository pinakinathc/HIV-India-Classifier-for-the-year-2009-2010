GVMC Urban Community Development wing Project Director D.V. Ramana Murthy, Superintendent Engineer (Projects) T. Moses Kumar and Medical Officer and Visakha AIDS Control Society Secretary K.S.L.G. Sastry were among those who received merit certificates for services from Tribal Welfare Minister P. Balaraju at the Independence Day celebration organised by the district administration at the Police Barracks. The GVMC tableau stood second among those displayed by various government departments.
