VIJAYAWADA: Members of the Krishna District AIDS Prevention Control Committee, who met here for a two-day workshop that ended on Wednesday, resolved to set up the facility of Integrated Counselling and Testing Centres (ICTCs) in 13 mandals across the district. The two-day event held at Vasavya Mahila Mandali was attended by representatives of various non-governmental organisations (NGOs) and government officials. There was also unanimity among members on seeking assistance of the members of Gynaecologists’ Association to ensure delivery of HIV-affected mothers in private clinics. The meeting also highlighted the need to train medical officers in primary health centres on sexually transmitted infections (STIs) while acknowledging the necessity to set up at least four new link anti-retroviral testing centres at Nandigama, Avanigadda, Bantumilli and Mylavaram, besides setting up drop-in centres at Machilipatnam, Avanigadda and Nuzvid. ADMHO (AIDS & Leprosy) T.V.S.N. Sastry presided over the workshop. Facilitator Srilatha, Sivalenka from Scientific Affairs Centre for Diseases Control, ART centre’s medical officer Dr. Sudhakar, Swagathi project programme manager Madhusudan, project officer Vinay, VMM president Chennupati Vidya and others participated.
