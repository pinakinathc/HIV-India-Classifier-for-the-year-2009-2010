Venugopala Rao, who has completed 30 research studies on a host of social issues and government schemes for various sections of people, was felicitated by Nandavareeka Association, founded by descendents of saint composer, Tallapaka Annamacharya, at a function here on Sunday. Dr. Rao's social research organisation, Foundation for Rural and Social Development (FRSD), conducted studies on the reach and efficacy of schemes/programmes like old age pension, HIV/AIDS awareness, employment, primary education, micro-irrigation, rural development, Panchayat Raj, agriculture, horticulture and housing.
