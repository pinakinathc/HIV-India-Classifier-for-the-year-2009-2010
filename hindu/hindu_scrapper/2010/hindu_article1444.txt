A 30-year-old HIV-positive woman has been elected member of a Block Development Committee (BDC) here to help her draw government attention to her plight and help her in treatment. Pravin Devi was elected member of the Gursan BDC here by an open panchayat in Tuksan village. The post would help her get free treatment for the HIV infection she contracted six months ago during blood transfusion at a nursing home in Aligarh, said Gram Pradhan Mukesh Kumar.- PTI
