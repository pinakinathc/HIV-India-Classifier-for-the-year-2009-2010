Delhi Health Minister Prof. Kiran Walia on Friday asked Delhiites to support the cause of women and children living with mental illness and HIV/AIDS. She was speaking at a function organised to mark International Human Rights Day at the UN headquarters on Lodhi Road here. The Minister stressed the need for providing care and support to this vulnerable section of society. More than 200 children from different schools participated in the function. “We as a society should guard against hatred and bitterness. Giving love and respect to others should be our mission,” said the Minister.
