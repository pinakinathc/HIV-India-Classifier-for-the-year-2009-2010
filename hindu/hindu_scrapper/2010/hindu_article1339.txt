TANSACS and Tamil Nadu Trust for HIV affected Children's awareness cycle rally on HIV/AIDS affected children will reach Salem on Wednesday. A press release says that the rally, flagged off at Coimbatore, after covering Tirupur and Erode, will reach Salem Integrated Collectorate on Wednesday morning. It will reach Chennai on December 1. Pamphlets will be issued on awareness and the Collector, J Chandrakumar, will receive it.
