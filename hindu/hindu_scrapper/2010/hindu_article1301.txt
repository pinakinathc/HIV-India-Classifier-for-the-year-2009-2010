The one-day training programme for master trainers of the Red Ribbon Clubs was organised by the district AIDS Control Society here on Saturday. District Collector M. Ravi Chandra, who was the chief guest, underscored the need for creating awareness among teenagers about HIV/AIDS. He also wanted the Red Ribbon Clubs to motivate students towards blood donation. Nodal officer of district AIDS Control Society M. Pavan Kumar and others were present. Earlier in the day, students of the Koringa Institute of Pharmacy and student forum of the Indian Pharmaceutical Association took out an anti-tobacco rally.
