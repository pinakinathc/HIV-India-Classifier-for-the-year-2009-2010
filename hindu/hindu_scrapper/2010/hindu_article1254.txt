SCMS-Cochin alumni have joined the current batches of students in a month-long social awareness campaign called “Parivarthana”. Management students will visit select government schools in the district and create awareness on a variety of topics relevant to the youngsters including misuse of technology and ways to prevent and fight AIDS, as part of the campaign that began on November 1. Students would plant a sapling during their visit. They would also implement a scheme to educate the Kudumbasree workers on the basics of accounting and management, according to a release. The social awareness programme would conclude on December 1. A rally and seminar will be held at Kalamassery. Elocution and drawing competitions will be held for representatives of the schools participating in the initiative, a pressnote informs.
