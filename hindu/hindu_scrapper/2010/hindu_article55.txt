Staff Reporter MALAPPURAM: The Life Bus awareness campaign, organised by the Kerala State AIDS Control Society, will come to an end in the district on Wednesday. The Life Bus started its campaign in the district at MES College, Mambad, on January 21. It covered 39 panchayats in the district. The Life Bus show will be held at Kunnumpuram on Wednesday afternoon, after covering Vengara and Parappur. The organisers said the campaign evoked warm response from the public. They said it played a key role in dispelling misconceptions about AIDS as well as HIV.
