The Population Services of India (PSI), in association with the Revised National Tuberculosis Control Programme (RNTCP), organised a workshop on Saturday at Bada Yermal village focussing on “Public private mix: achieving international standards of TB care” for doctors practising in Udupi taluk. A press release issued here on Monday stated that 15 doctors from the private sector participated in the workshop. It was organised under the Edde Arogya Project, an integrated health project focussing on HIV/AIDS, TB and diarrheal prevention catering to 57,000 people in 11 villages near the Suzlon Special Economic Zone (SEZ) in Udupi taluk. District TB Officer B. Vishwanath and Area Programme Manager of PSI Abhilash Philip participated in the workshop.
