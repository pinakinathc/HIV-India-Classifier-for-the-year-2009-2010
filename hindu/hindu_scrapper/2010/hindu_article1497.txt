Nearly 500 rallyists participating in the Great Indian Ride arrived in the city on Wednesday and conducted a public awareness drive on the imperatives of wearing a helmet while riding. The rally, sponsored by Mahindra Two Wheelers, was flagged off from Kanyakumari and will terminate at Nagpur. On arrival at Mysore, the rallyists were received by Nagesh Preetham of SNP Motors, who used the opportunity to conduct a public awareness drive to mark World AIDS Day. The rally was flagged off by H.S. Shankarlinge Gowda. It passed through the main thoroughfares of the city and terminated at the tollgate on the Bangalore-Mysore Highway.
