As part of the Tamil Nadu State AIDS Control Society (TANSACS) youth festival, a district-level inter-collegiate sports meet will be conducted at the district stadium here from July 23 to 25. Those in the age group of 18 to 29 years are eligible to participate . Athletics, basketball, volleyball and kabaddi will be conducted for men and women, while football and hockey will be held for men. Entries will close on July 22. Further details can be obtained by dialling 04329-294395.
