Staff Correspondent DHARWAD: The eighth Samskrutika Janotsava, a three-day event organised by the All-India Democratic Students Organisation (AIDSO) and All-India Democratic Youth Organisation (AIDYO), began here on Saturday. Inaugurating the event, writer Buddanna Hingamire lauded the initiative and said that festivals such as the janotsava which promoted progressive ideas should be encouraged. Writer Jagadish Mangalurmath described the event as a “festival of understanding oneself”. State secretary of AIDYO Ramanjanappa Aldalli underscored the need for developing an alternative cultural movement. AIDYO district unit president Laxman Jadagannavar made introductory remarks. AIDSO district unit president Gangadhar Badiger presided over the function.
