Volunteers of the NSS unit and the Red Ribbon Club of Shri Venkateswara Matric H.S. School, Thanjavur, visited the Red Ribbon Express train that came to Thanjavur recently and organised anti-AIDS awareness rally at the railway station. G. Jaishankar, principal-cum-programme officer of NSS, took the students to visit the Red Ribbon Express and gathered more information on AIDS awareness. He also flagged off the rally and the students carried placards about the precautionary measures to be taken on AIDS. The volunteers also distributed pamphlets to the people. S.P. Balasubramanian, correspondent of the school, S. Gowri, senior principal, appreciated the efforts of the NSS and Red Ribbon Club volunteers.
