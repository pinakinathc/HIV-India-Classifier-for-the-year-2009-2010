N icholas Productions is auditioning for its upcoming production “Rent”, slated to be performed this December. Those interested in acting, singing, dancing, stage management, creative designing, marketing or backstage, can audition on September 20 and 21 at Kaveri Complex, Nungambakkam, between 6 p.m. and 10 p.m. Rent is a rock opera, based on the lives of artists with AIDS, and their struggle through life. The musical will be performed in association with a Chennai-based NGO spreading awareness about AIDS, and will be performed during World AIDS Week.  For details, call 98840-93510.
