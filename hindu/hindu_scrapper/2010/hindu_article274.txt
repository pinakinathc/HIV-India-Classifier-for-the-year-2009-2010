Staff Reporter KARUR: Collector J. Uma Maheswari lauded the efforts and the aim of a differently-abled youth Nellai Nelson who has been undertaking a campaign on tricycle to create awareness on the AIDS/HIV disease.The Collector invited the youth to her chamber and offered felicitations for the success of his endeavour. She also presented him with a wrist watch and sunglasses. Nelson, who started off from Dindigul on October 21, last year has so far covered Coimbatore, Tirupur, Erode, Salem and Namakkal districts before touring Karur over the past few days.Nelson hoped to complete his journey by May at Chennai after touring Tiruchi, Perambalur, Villupuram and Thiruvallur districts.
