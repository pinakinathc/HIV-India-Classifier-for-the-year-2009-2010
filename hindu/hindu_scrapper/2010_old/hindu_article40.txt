Staff Correspondent Udupi: Deputy Commissioner P. Hemalatha said on Wednesday that the Red Ribbon Express, conceptualised by the Rajiv Gandhi Foundation, would be stationed at the Udupi railway station on February 18 and 19. Chairing a preparatory meeting, Ms. Hemalatha said Karnataka was one of the six States in the country which had high incidences of HIV/AIDS. The aim of the Red Ribbon Express was to create awareness among people, especially the youth, about HIV/AIDS, she said.
