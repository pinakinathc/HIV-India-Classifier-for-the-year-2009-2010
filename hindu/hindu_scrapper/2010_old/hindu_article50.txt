A Correspondent KALPETTA: Spreading awareness of HIV-prevention steps, Life Bus, a campaign vehicle of the Kerala State AIDS Control Society, will start its one-week tour of Wayanad district on Friday. District Collector T. Bhaskaran will flag off the campaign at the auditorium of the Oriental School of Hotel Management, Lakkidi. District Medical Officer K.C. Preetha told presspersons here on Wednesday that the bus would stop at 25 grama panchayats and one municipality, visiting every day three centres where video shows will be screened and artistes of the Nehru Yuvak Kendra will stage cultural programmes. Dr. Preetha said the campaign also aimed at mitigating social discrimination of HIV-infected people. The Health Department and the Prathyasa Kendra are part of the campaign.
