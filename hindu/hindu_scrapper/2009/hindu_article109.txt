VIZIANAGARAM: Collector G. Ram Narayana Reddy inaugurated the three-day workshop on Plan of Action 2009-10 for AIDS control in the district on Monday. Deputy Director of APSACS Mekala, Regional Coordinator Chetan Pillai, Project Officer G. Adilingam and others attended the meeting. The Collector asked the field experts group to prepare a plan of action in such a manner that the prevalence of HIV positive cases dropped from the present 0.6 per cent to the national average of 0.3 per cent. The State average is 0.8 per cent.
