Staff Correspondent HUBLI: “Aavishkara Pragatipara Samskrutika Vedike”, All India Democratic Youth Organisation (AIDYO) and All India Democratic Students Organisation (AIDSO) are organising a four-day Dharwad Samskrutika Janotsava in Dharwad and Hubli from Sunday. In a release issued here on behalf of the three organisations, H.G. Desai has said that it was the seventh edition of the janotsava and this time, a few programmes would be conducted in Hubli. He said that the objective of the cultural event was to sensitise the youngsters on the developments around them through “good entertainment”. The janotsava programmes, which include dance and music programmes, plays and screening of films, will be held for three days in Dharwad and for one day in Hubli.
