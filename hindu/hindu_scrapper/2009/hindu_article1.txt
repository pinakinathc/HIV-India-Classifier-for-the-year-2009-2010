World AIDS Day was observed by the Junior Red Cross (JRC) members of  . Various programmes were organised to create awareness among students about AIDS. The JRC members took oath for creating an AIDS-free society. Headmaster M.I. Mohammed Ali, JRC counsellor M. Jawahar Hasan and school advisor I. Badarusada spoke. Mohideen Abdul Khadar won the first prize in elocution and Uvaiz the third prize in poetry at the district-level contest conducted by the Indian Red Cross Society.
