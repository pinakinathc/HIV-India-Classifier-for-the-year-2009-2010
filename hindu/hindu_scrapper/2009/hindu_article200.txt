BERHAMPUR: The activists of the Centre for Disease Control (CDC) of Atlanta in America will work among the HIV positive persons in the Ganjam district of Orissa. The organisation works under the Health Department of the United States of America. It may be noted that Ganjam has the highest incidence of HIV positive persons in Orissa. The members of the organisation would work through the Lepra India and train women in rural areas especially the Self-Help Groups regarding steps to be taken for the rehabilitation, treatment and protection of HIV-infected persons.
