Staff Reporter TIRUCHI: An AIDS awareness programme was conducted for the Railway Protection Force (RPF) and Railway Protection Special Force (RPSF) personnel at the RPF Training Centre in Khajamalai here on Tuesday. T.K.S. Senthil Kumar, founder and secretary of Anbalayam, a service organisation, and other resource persons from the organisation spoke about AIDS and the ways to prevent the disease. A short film on AIDS was screened on the occasion. The programme was inaugurated by Chief Medical Superintendent, Railway Hospital, Ponmalai, Dr. Velusamy. Divisional Security Commissioner, RPF, Tiruchi S.R. Gandhi, Commandant, RPSF, Tiruchi, T.S. Raju and about 300 RPF and RPSF personnel took part.
