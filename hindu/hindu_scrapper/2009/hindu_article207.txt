Staff Reporter Nagercoil: United Nations Development Programme, in association with a non-governmental organisation, based at Alanchi would conduct a training programme for ‘Red Ribbon’ Club members attached to three schools in Kanyakumari district on Friday (February 27) in order to create awareness among the school children on issues related to HIV/AIDS. The schools are Sethu Lekshmi Bai Higher Secondary School, Duthie Girls’ Higher Secondary School and St Alosiyus High School, Vetturnimadam. Manager of Tamil Nadu AIDS Control Society Suresh Kumar, film director P.C.Anbhazagan, Chief Educational Officer C.Ponniah, director of Vasantham Trust Chandra Kala and others will participate.
