KARIMNAGAR: On the occasion of the National Leprosy Eradication Day on January 30, the District Medical and Health Department decided to conduct essay writing competitions for school and college students on January 24 at District Additional DM&HO (TB and Leprosy) office at Bhagathnagar in Karimnagar. The topics for the essay writing competitions are ‘Role of youth in eradication of Leprosy’ and ‘Role of youth in the prevention of HIV\AIDS’. For the school students, the competitions would be held at 11 a.m. and for college students at 2 p.m.. –Staff Reporter
