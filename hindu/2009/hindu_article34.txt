TIRUPATI: A ten-day NSS camp by volunteers of Sree Vidyanikethan Degree College that began last Friday is under way at Bandarlapalle village of Chandragiri mandal. After planting saplings on the first day, a free medical camp was conducted in the village, after which awareness programmes were held on preventing AIDS, importance of education, maintaining nutritional values and controlling contagious diseases.
