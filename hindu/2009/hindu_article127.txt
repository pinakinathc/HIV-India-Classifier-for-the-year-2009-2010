Pix has bagged the official telecast rights of the BAFTA Awards 2009. Pix will showcase the entire event live from the Royal Opera House in London at 5.30 a.m on February 9. A repeat telecast will be aired at 10 p.m. on the same day. As part of the upcoming event, Pix will screen a BAFTA movie-marathon till February 9. It will include movies like the AIDS drama “Philadelphia”, “Trauma” and“Chicago” . Steven Spielberg’s “Close Encounters of the Third Kind” will be aired on February 8 at 11.30 p.m while “The Queen” starring Helen Mirren about the tumultuous week following the death of Lady Diana in a car crash will be aired on February 9 at 8 p.m.
