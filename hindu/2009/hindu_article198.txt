The students of   donated Rs. 3.96 lakhs to the India Development Foundation (IDF) for implementing various projects for the rehabilitation of leprosy, tuberculosis and HIV / AIDS patients. The Principal of the school, Rev. Bro. Jesuraj, handed over the cheque to C. Chidambaram, manager, Southern, IDF, and the senior field officer, Sam Deva Asir. They both spoke on tuberculosis, its symptoms, diagnosis, treatment, prevention and urged the students to have a concern always for those afflicted by the condition. Chidambaram thanked the Principal, staff and students of the school for their gesture and said that this was the biggest donation made by a school to the Foundation. Bro. Jesuraj hailed the activities of the IDF. The students who collected the highest amount were honoured.
