M. Sudhakar, NSS Programme Officer, RVR & JC College of Engineering has been conferred state-level award at the 41st NSS Foundation Day celebrations. He received the award from the Principal Secretary in the presence of Vice-Chancellors of JNTU-H, SVU. Mr. Sudhakar has been credited with establishing the NSS unit at RVR College, setting up the biggest blood donor club, conducting the HIV-AIDS programmes. NSS Coordinator, ANU, N. Vijaya Ratnam complimented Mr. Sudhakar for receiving the honour.
