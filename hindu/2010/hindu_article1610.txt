District Collector K. Ramgopal on Wednesday exhorted bankers to provide a loan of Rs. 15,000 to Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV) carriers to eke out a living. Addressing a bankers meet at the DRDA conference hall here, he said the government would provide Rs.10,000 as grant to HIV carriers from DRDA funds, for self-employment. All those undergoing anti retroviral therapywould be provided Rs. 200 per month.
