Adolescent education (Kaumara Vidya) programmes will be organised for Class X students in all government and Zilla Parishad high schools in the district from November 29 to December 1. In a statement, principal of the District Institute of Education and Training (DIET) K. Adinarayana Murthy said that the sessions would be conducted for 16 hours from November 29 to December 1 and then from December 6 to 8 in the entire district. The programme would cover the prescribed topics, including life skills, reproductive organs, personal hygiene, RTI & STI, Prevention of HIV/AIDS and ill-effects of drug abuse. Quiz, essay-writing, debates and drawing competitions on life skills would also be organised as part of the programme.
