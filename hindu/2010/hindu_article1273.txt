A Deepavali celebration for HIV-infected children and those of parents having HIV/AIDS was held with grandeur here on Sunday. The event was organised by the Lions Club of Tirupur Knitcity, Native Medicare Charitable Trust (NMCT) and Tirupur Sewa Samithi. Mayor K. Selvaraj distributed sweets and dresses to around 500 children after inaugurating the event. Cultural programmes by children also marked the occasion.
