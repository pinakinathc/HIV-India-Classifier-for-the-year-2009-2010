A Centre for Renewable Energy research and new home for people living with HIV/AIDS would be coming up shortly in the premises of James College of Engineering and technology Navalkadu, near Nagercoil and James Hospital premises at Colachel respectively Governor Surjit Singh Barnala lighted the inaugural lamp for the two programmes initiated by James Prem Kumar, chairman and managing director of the institution, at Kanyakumari.. The chairman said research in new and renewable energy was the need of the hour especially when the country was facing acute power shortage. The research centre will provide sophisticated facilities for the scholars and students, who wished to contribute to the cause, would be awarded special fellowships. James Hospital, which also housed a home for people living with HIV/AIDS for the past several years, will construct a new building in the hospital premises.
