As many as 1,095 children hailing from poor families, most of who were affected by or suffering from AIDS, were given clothes and nutritious food worth Rs. 3 lakh at a function in Vasavya Mahila Mandali on Thursday. Additional District Medical and Health Officer (AIDS) T.V.S.N. Sastry, who participated as chief guest, lauded the initiative and underscored the need for extending a helping hand to the children in distress.
