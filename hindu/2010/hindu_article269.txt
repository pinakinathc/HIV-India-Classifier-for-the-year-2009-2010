Staff Reporter PUDUCHERRY: The Red Ribbon Club of Rajiv Gandhi Arts and Science College, Government of Puducherry, conducted an AIDS awareness rally on March 18. Principal of the college V.P. Seetharaman flagged off the rally. Monitoring and Evaluation Officer, Pondicherry AIDS Control Society D. Balamurugan participated in the programme and advised students to have a complete understanding about HIV/AIDS and spread awareness in their respective villages, according to a press release. The rally, which started from the college campus at Thavalakuppam, went through Thanampalayam, Andiarpalayam, Korukanmedu and reached Nallavadu-Thavalakuppam junction. Around 200 students and staff participated in the rally, added the release.
