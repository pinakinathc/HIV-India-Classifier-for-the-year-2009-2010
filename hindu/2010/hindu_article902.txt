West Bengal Governor M.K. Narayanan will arrive here on a two-day visit to the district on Thursday. He will inaugurate a function organised jointly by Daya Charitable Trust and the Janamaitri project of the Kerala Police to adopt 150 widows afflicted with HIV\AIDS, at the Town Hall here on Friday. Daya Charitable Trust chairman P. Sanjumon said Onam kits would be distributed to 50 visually challenged persons and school uniforms would be distributed to 155 students of the Sreekrishnapuram School for Blind at the function. In the evening, the Governor would be given a reception at Ambat Tharavad, Chittur, by his family members and friends.
