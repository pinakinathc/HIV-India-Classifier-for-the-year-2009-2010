A free health camp was organised by BSES Rajdhani Power Limited (BRPL) and Delhi AIDS Control Society at Paschim Vihar here over the weekend. A team of doctors and specialists along with counsellors from Delhi State AIDS Control Society offered free tests and consultations at the camp. A “Red Ribbon Van” was deployed to spread awareness on AIDS and its prevention. Pamphlets were also distributed and a “nukkad natak” staged as part of the programme.
