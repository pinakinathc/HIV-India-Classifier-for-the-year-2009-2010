Lepra India launched a special project to check spread of HIV in Orvakal, Midtur and Peapuly mandals of Kurnool district. Addressing a coordination meeting in this connection here on Wednesday, Collector Ramsankar Naik said the project should be extended to all areas of the district. He said the camp held at Atmakur for Chenchus indicated that malnutrition was the major cause of incidence of serious infections.
