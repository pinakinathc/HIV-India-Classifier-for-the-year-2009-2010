The Bangalore Schools Sports Foundation, in association with Sneha Care Home and Shining Star School, will host Champion in Me 2010, a two-day sports and cultural event for children living with HIV (CLHIV), at Snehadaan on Sarjapur Road in the city on December 11 and 12. Sneha Care Home director Fr. Mathew told presspersons on Friday that Champion in Me was a well-designed sports-based initiative aimed at providing a holistic approach to physical and mental well-being of CLHIV.
