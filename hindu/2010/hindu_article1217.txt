Number of deaths due to sudden cardiac arrest is far more than due to breast cancer, AIDS, lung cancer or stroke and to create awareness among the people of the city the Indus Hospitals has begun an awareness camp on its hospital premises. A release from the hospital said that a free screening cardiac camp to identify individuals at high risk of sudden cardiac death was being conducted during the month of October. The hospital was also conducting cardiac resuscitation (CPR) training to high-risk individuals and social organisations.
