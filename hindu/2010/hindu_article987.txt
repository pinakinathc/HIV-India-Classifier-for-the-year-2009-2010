P. Venu Gopal, Associate Professor of Paediatrics, Andhra Medical College, has been appointed Nodal Officer of ART (Anti Retroviral Therapy) Centre of King George Hospital. He assumed charge on Friday. He said that the number of HIV cases reported at the ART Centre was between 150 and 200 a day. Of these, 20 per cent was paediatric cases. New drugs, that have increased the longevity of patients, were available and they would be given to patients as per the guidelines of the World Health Organisation, he said.
