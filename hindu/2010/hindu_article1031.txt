Bangalore District Committees of All India Democratic Youth Organisation (AIDYO) and All India Democratic Students' Organisation (AIDSO) will stage a protest demonstration on Thursday, against the Department of Employment and Training for conducting re-examination for accounts and science subjects for II year Fitter and Electrician Trades conducted by the Industrial Training Institute during July 2010. The demonstration programme will be held at the Office of the Commissioner, Department of Employment and Training, Kaushalya Bhavan, near Dairy Circle at 11 a.m.
