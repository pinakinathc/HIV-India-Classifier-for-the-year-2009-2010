Staff Reporter MALAPPURAM: The Red Ribbon Express, a joint initiative of the Central government and the National AIDS Control Organisation aimed at raising awareness on HIV/AIDS, arrived at Tirur on Saturday. The train will remain at Tirur railway station on Sunday. The public will have free entry into the train. Union Minister of State for Railways E. Ahamed inaugurated the message rally at Tirur on Saturday. P.P. Abdulla Kutty, MLA, presided over the function. E.T. Mohammed Basheer, MP; K. Kutty Ahamed Kutty, MLA; Abdurahman Randathani, MLA; Arimbra Mohammed, district panchayat president; and M.C. Mohandas, District Collector, addressed the gathering.
