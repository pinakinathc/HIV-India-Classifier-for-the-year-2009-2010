Members of All India Democratic Students' Organisation (AIDSO) have resolved to launch an intensified agitation to protest against the commercialisation of education in the country. At the AIDSO 4th State-level meeting held in Dharwad recently, the member-students discussed their problems at length and adopted several resolutions. These resolutions include opposition against commoditisation of education; Foreign Education Provider's Bill 2010; Private Universities Bill; increase in fee and bus pass prices; irregularities, corruption and favouritism in universities; semester system; to check deterioration of cultural values; inflation and land-grabbing. The members also demanded that the Government provide basic amenities in schools, colleges and hostels.
