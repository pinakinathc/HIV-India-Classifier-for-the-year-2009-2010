A mobile Integrated Counselling and Testing Centre (ICTC) was inaugurated on the premises of chilli yard on Wednesday. The centre was set up as part of Subham-3 by the Population Services Organisation working towards prevention and raising awareness on the spread of HIV-AIDS. Over 150 persons turned up for voluntary testing services at the ICTC. District AIDS Control Society project manager Lankapalli Madhusundan Rao who inaugurated the centre asked the workers at the yard to utilise the facilities at the ICTC. Voluntary testing was one of the best methods in HIV-AIDS prevention, he observed. Population Services Organisation communication officer Samba was also present.
