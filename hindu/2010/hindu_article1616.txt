W ith the Christmas festivities in full swing Novotel Hyderabad Convention Centre is spreading Christmas cheer with some very special guests. On December 23, the children from Nireekshana NGO were invited for festive celebrations. Nireekshana ACET (AIDS Care, Education and Treatment) is a non-profit organisation. After a hearty welcome from Santa, the Chef took children to the Gingerbread House for a storytelling session. The entire staff of the hotel came together to make the day special with a host of festive activities including Christmas carols and a special dance by Santa Claus.
