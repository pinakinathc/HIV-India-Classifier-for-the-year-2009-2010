Adilabad Collector P. Venkateswarlu inaugurated a tribal women literacy centre at Belsary Rampur village of Tamsi mandal late on Friday. The centre, a pilot project meant to improve rate of literacy among tribal women, is run jointly by the Adult Education Department, SAIDS NGO and the Jana Shikshan Sansthan.
