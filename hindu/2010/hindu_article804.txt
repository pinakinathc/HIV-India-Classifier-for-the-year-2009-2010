District AIDS prevention officer Chinnanagappa has expressed concern over the increasing number of HIV cases in Hassan district. He said that 100 new cases are detected every month. Mr. Chinnanagappa was speaking at a seminar on “Role of youth in prevention of HIV/AIDS” at the Government PU College in Harnahalli on Thursday. The seminar was organised by the district information office. Mr. Chinnanagappa said that more cases were reported among people in the 15 to 24 age group. He said that his department had started several red ribbon clubs at the college level to create awareness on HIV/AIDS.
