District AIDS Prevention and Control Unit proposed to start Red Ribbon clubs in 287 colleges in the district. A one-day training programme for lecturers of degree and junior colleges was organised here on Saturday to motivate them to start the clubs with an objective of creating awareness about HIV/AIDS, life skills and voluntary donation. In the next phase the master trainers will organise training sessions in the colleges. Additional DMHO Raja Subba Rao, vice-principal Sharma, and others were present.
