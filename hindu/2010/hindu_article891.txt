Besides providing treatment, counselling should be given to AIDS patients so that they may live a normal life on par with other members of the society, said M.S.Shanmugham, District Collector here on Tuesday. Speaking at a meeting of workers of the AIDS control scheme, the Collector said that immediate treatment should be given to HIV infected persons who come to AIDS centres for combined treatment functioning at government hospitals and primary health centres in the district. Voluntary organisations and the government should jointly implement schemes and programmes to help in the rehabilitation of those affected. Mathivanan, Deputy Director, Health, participated in the meeting, said a release issued here on Tuesday.
