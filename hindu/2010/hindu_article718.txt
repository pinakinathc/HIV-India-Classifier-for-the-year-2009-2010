Majji Guru Murty Service Society (MGMSS) has conducted a free medical camp at Botcha Guru Naidu Memorial MP UP School at Thotapalem and gave away medicines free of cost after examining 300 persons. Botcha Jhansi Lakshmi, MP, inaugurated the camp. She asked the society members to create awareness among people about AIDS, swine flu and seasonal diseases. Medical practitioners Chitti Ramana Rao, Shiva Ram and G.B. Venkat examined the patients.
