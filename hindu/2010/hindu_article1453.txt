Pydah Degree College initiated the Red Ribbon Club programme on its campus here on Saturday. The club was inaugurated by the Deputy Superintendent of King George Hospital P. Shyam Prasad. In his inaugural address, he explained the importance of donating blood. He also dealt with the awareness campaign, concerning the spread of HIV/AIDS. Principal G. Sivarama Krishna, Vice-Principal D.P. Raju and master trainer M.F. Rahman were present.
