Over 700 National Cadet Corps cadets from various schools took out a rally on Sunday from Madura College Higher Secondary School to Setupati Higher Secondary School to create awareness of HIV. It was flagged off by Lt. Col. Issac P. Therattil, Officiating Commander, 2 Tamil Nadu Medical Company. Mohan Kumar, NCC Officer, Madurai Medical College, addressed the cadets about the nature and causes of HIV infection and its prevention at the culmination of the rally.
