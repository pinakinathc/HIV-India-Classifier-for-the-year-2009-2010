Staff Reporter TIRUPATI: The NSS inter-collegiate special camp conducted on SV University campus came to a grand close on Friday. Around 200 volunteers from various colleges cleaned thorny bushes and shrubs near the hostel blocks, library and administrative building. In his address, Vice-Chancellor N. Prabhakara Rao advised them be socially responsible and utilise their ability to address individual and community problems. Registrar J. Pratap Reddy asked the volunteers to shoulder responsibility of relief and rehabilitation during natural disasters. NSS programme coordinator R. Sreenivasulu Naidu explained that shramadan, intellectual sessions, AIDS awareness meets and yoga classes were conducted during the weeklong programme.
