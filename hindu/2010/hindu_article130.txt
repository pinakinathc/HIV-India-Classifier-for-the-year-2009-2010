Staff Reporter Nagercoil: The Red Ribbon Express would reach Nagercoil on March 13 to create awareness on HIV/AIDS among the people particularly the younger generation. The Red Ribbon Express was flagged off by Congress leader Sonia Gandhi on December 1, last year in New Delhi and it was organised by the National AIDS control organisation and State AIDS control society. The train would halt for two days at Nagercoil Railway Station. The Collector, Rajendra Ratnoo has appealed to the people from all walks of life including students and Government employees to pay a visit to the train.
