Vasavya Mahila Mandali organised a meeting to distribute books and uniforms to poor and destitute children suffering from HIV, here on Thursday. On the occasion, Mayor M.V. Ratna Bindu said that free seats in corporate colleges would be provided to all students who secured more than 500 marks in SSC. Indian Medical Association president G. Samaram, Mandali president Chennupati Vidya and others spoke.
