Staff Reporter Kozhikode: OISCA-International invites applications from candidates to the posts of ‘project co-ordinator’ and ‘counsellors’ in the ‘Surakhsha’ project. The project is aimed at controlling the spread of HIV/AIDS among drug addicts and migrant labourers and is being implemented with support from the Kerala State Aids Control Society. Applicants should be MSW or a postgraduate in the social service stream, a press release issued here on Sunday said. Counsellors should know languages spoken in other States or be proficient in de-addiction counselling. Applications, with copies of certificates and other relevant details, should be addressed to the Project Director, OISCA Migrant Suraksha, OISCA Youth Centre Building, U.K.S. Road, Calicut 673001. For more details contact 90482 06758.
